Our Facilities

Harvard University offers a wide array of facilities, including libraries, museums, athletic centers, research labs, and gardens, catering to academic, research, and recreational needs.

Harry Elkins Widener Memorial Library

Harvard University Library, the oldest library system in the US and the world's largest academic library, boasts over 20 million volumes, 400 million manuscripts, and extensive digital resources, accessible through the HOLLIS catalog.

Harvard Catalyst

Harvard Catalyst catalyzes research across all clinical and translational domains by providing investigators with opportunities such as pilot funding, free resources such as biostatistics consultations, training and mentoring programs, and numerous courses.

Malkin Athletic Center

The Malkin Athletic Center (MAC) is Harvard University's primary recreation facility, a five-story building at 39 Holyoke Street in Cambridge, MA, housing varsity sports like volleyball, fencing, and wrestling, along with various recreational amenities.
